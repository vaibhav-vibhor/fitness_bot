📚 Health Metrics Reference

🫁 SpO2 (Blood Oxygen Saturation)

  • Normal: 95-100%
  • Low: Below 95% (consult healthcare provider)
  • Note: Measured during sleep on compatible devices

🌬️ Breathing Rate

  • Normal Adult: 12-20 breaths per minute
  • Higher: May indicate stress or illness
  • Lower: May indicate good fitness or deep sleep

🌡️ Temperature Variation

  • Range: Personal baseline variation in °C
  • Changes: Can indicate stress, illness, or menstrual cycle
  • Tracking: Relative to your personal average

❤️ VO2 Max (Cardio Fitness)

  • Excellent: 50+ (men), 45+ (women)
  • Good: 43-50 (men), 36-45 (women)
  • Fair: 35-42 (men), 28-35 (women)
  • Poor: Below 35 (men), Below 28 (women)
