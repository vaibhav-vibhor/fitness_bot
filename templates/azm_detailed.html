💡 About Active Zone Minutes

Active Zone Minutes are earned for time spent in fat burn, cardio, or peak heart rate zones.

  • Fat Burn Zone: 50-69% of max heart rate - burns fat, improves endurance
  • Cardio Zone: 70-84% of max heart rate - improves cardiovascular fitness
  • Peak Zone: 85-100% of max heart rate - improves performance and power

Goal: Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate intensity or 75 minutes of vigorous intensity per week (WHO guidelines)
